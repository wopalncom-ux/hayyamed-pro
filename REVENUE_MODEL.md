# Hayya Med Pro — Revenue Model

## Revenue Philosophy

Hayya Med Pro generates recurring, compounding revenue across multiple streams. Every stream is designed to increase LTV, reduce churn, and create defensible moats that make the platform sticky for both individuals and institutions.

---

## Stream 1: Individual Subscriptions (Core ARR)

| Plan | Price | Billing | Target |
|---|---|---|---|
| Free | $0 | — | Acquisition funnel |
| Pro | $49 | Annual | Individual professionals |
| Premium | $99 | Annual | High-volume professionals, locum doctors |
| Enterprise Individual | Custom | Annual | Expat doctors with multi-country needs |

**ARR Projection:**
- 1,000 Pro subscribers = $49,000 ARR
- 5,000 Pro subscribers = $245,000 ARR
- 10,000 Pro subscribers = $490,000 ARR

**Upsell path:** Free → Pro (PDF reports, email alerts) → Premium (multi-country, advanced analytics)

---

## Stream 2: Employer Packages (High-Value ARR)

| Package | Price | Target |
|---|---|---|
| Employer Starter | $199/year | Clinics (up to 10 staff) |
| Employer Growth | $499/year | Mid-size clinics (up to 50 staff) |
| Hospital Package | $2,000–$10,000/year | Hospital groups (100–1,000+ staff) |
| Healthcare Group | Custom | Multi-hospital groups |
| Government Agency | Custom | MOH, QCHP, health authorities |

**ARR Projection:**
- 10 Hospital contracts × $5,000 avg = $50,000 ARR
- 50 Hospital contracts × $5,000 avg = $250,000 ARR
- 5 Government contracts × $20,000 avg = $100,000 ARR

---

## Stream 3: University & Academic Packages

| Package | Price | Target |
|---|---|---|
| University Basic | $3,000/year | Single medical faculty |
| University Standard | $8,000/year | Multi-faculty university |
| University Enterprise | $20,000+/year | Medical university group |

**Value proposition:** Universities can issue verified CME credits directly through the platform, track alumni compliance, and report to licensing authorities automatically.

---

## Stream 4: Training Provider Marketplace

| Fee | Structure |
|---|---|
| Listing fee | $200–$500/year per provider |
| Transaction commission | 5–10% per course enrollment |
| Featured placement | $100–$500/month |
| Verified provider badge | $300/year |

**Market:** Medical conferences, online CME providers, simulation centers, hospital training departments.

---

## Stream 5: White-Label Licensing

Target: Government health authorities, large hospital groups, medical councils.

| License Type | Price |
|---|---|
| Regional white-label | $50,000–$200,000/year |
| National platform license | $200,000–$1M+/year |
| Source code license | Custom |

**Example:** QCHP licenses the platform as the official Qatar CME tracking portal. Hayya Med Pro receives a national licensing fee + per-professional fee.

---

## Stream 6: Sponsorship & Pharma/MedDevice Programs

| Stream | Model |
|---|---|
| CME program sponsorship | Pharma sponsors accredited CME activities on platform |
| Product training programs | Medical device companies sponsor mandatory training |
| Branded learning paths | Pharma co-brands specialty education tracks |

**Compliance note:** All sponsored content must be independently accredited and disclosed. Content is editorial; sponsors fund but do not control.

---

## Stream 7: Data & Analytics (Future — Year 2+)

Anonymized, aggregated healthcare workforce intelligence reports for:
- Health ministries (workforce planning)
- Medical schools (curriculum alignment)
- Pharma companies (HCP training trends)

**Model:** Annual intelligence subscription ($5,000–$50,000/report)

---

## Revenue Mix Target (Year 3)

| Stream | % of ARR |
|---|---|
| Individual subscriptions | 30% |
| Employer packages | 35% |
| University & academic | 15% |
| Training marketplace | 10% |
| White-label & government | 5% |
| Sponsorship | 5% |

---

## Key Revenue Metrics to Track

- MRR / ARR
- Net Revenue Retention (NRR) — target >110%
- Churn rate — target <5%/year for Pro, <2%/year for Employer
- LTV:CAC ratio — target >3:1
- Months to payback — target <12 months
- Average Revenue Per User (ARPU)
- Enterprise contract value (ACV)
