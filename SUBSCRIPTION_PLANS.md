## Pricing Principles

1. **Price to value, not to cost.** A missed license renewal costs $1,000–$10,000. We charge $49.
2. **Annual-only billing.** Monthly billing increases churn. Annual billing locks in ARR.
3. **Local pricing by country.** Qatar/UAE: USD. Saudi: SAR. Egypt: EGP. India: INR.
4. **No credit card for Free.** Lowest friction acquisition.
5. **Employer buys for team.** Employer plan covers the employer's seat + all linked staff.
