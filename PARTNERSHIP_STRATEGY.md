# Hayya Med Pro — Partnership Strategy

## Partnership Philosophy

Partnerships are the fastest path to enterprise ARR and defensible market position. Every partnership should deliver one or more of: distribution, data, credibility, revenue, or regulatory access.

---

## Tier 1: Government & Regulatory Authorities

### Target: QCHP (Qatar Council for Healthcare Practitioners)

**Why:** QCHP is the gatekeeper for every licensed healthcare professional in Qatar. An official QCHP integration or endorsement makes Hayya Med Pro the de facto standard.

| Opportunity | Model | Value |
|---|---|---|
| Official CME tracking partner | API integration with QCHP portal | Distribution to all Qatar HCPs |
| White-label QCHP portal | License Hayya Med Pro as QCHP's system | $200,000–$500,000/year |
| QCHP activity import | Pull verified activities from QCHP into our platform | Product stickiness |

**Approach:** Relationship-first. Demonstrate the platform. Offer free institutional access to QCHP staff. Build trust before proposing commercial terms.

### Other Regulatory Targets (Year 2–3)

| Authority | Country | Opportunity |
|---|---|---|
| SCFHS | Saudi Arabia | National CME system partnership |
| DHA | Dubai | Dubai-licensed professional platform |
| DOH | Abu Dhabi | Abu Dhabi HCP compliance portal |
| MOH Kuwait | Kuwait | National tracking system |
| NHRA | Bahrain | Official CME registry integration |

### Target Profile
- Hospital groups with 200+ licensed staff
- Multi-specialty hospitals in Qatar, Saudi, UAE
- International hospital brands operating in GCC (Johns Hopkins Aramco, Cleveland Clinic Abu Dhabi, Sidra Medicine)

### Value Proposition
"Your HR team currently tracks CME compliance manually in Excel. We automate it completely. Your staff are notified automatically. You get real-time compliance dashboards. Licensing authority audit reports are one click."

### Partnership Models

| Model | Structure | Revenue |
|---|---|---|
| Enterprise license | Annual contract, unlimited staff | $5,000–$50,000/year |
| Preferred platform | Employer endorses Hayya Med Pro to staff | Individual Pro subscriptions |

| Data integration | HRIS → Hayya Med Pro API sync | API access fee |

## Partnership Development Process

1. **Identify** — Tier and target from the lists above
2. **Research** — Decision maker, current pain, budget cycle
3. **Relationship** — Meeting, demo, free trial for their team
4. **Proposal** — Commercial proposal with clear ROI
5. **Pilot** — 3-month paid pilot with measurable success criteria
6. **Contract** — Annual contract with renewal terms
7. **Expand** — Add more features, more departments, more countries
